Clinical & Patient Information

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I get the BedalPatch?

Bedal Patch is exclusively distributed by Jigsaw Medical LLC and can be purchased by visiting our web store at www.bedalpatchusa.com.  Medical providers may order directly through 630-587-0019.  

Can the BedalPatch be used by people with sensitive skin?

Bedal Patch is gentle on delicate skin and can be safely used on a daily basis. However, if you experience inflammation or discomfort please discontinue use.

Do I have to remove or change my existing primary dressing to use the BedalPatch?

No,  BedalPatch will go over your primary dressing and easily allow for multiple lumens or ports to be safely tucked into the BedalPatch.

Can the BedalPatch be applied without assistance?

We recommend patients have assistance with at least its first application to ensure proper placement and adhesion.  However, many patients are able to apply the BedalPatch independently.

 

Is there a video available to show how to put the BedalPatch on?

Yes please click on this link to our instructional video. 

Who invented the Bedal Patch and why did it take so long for someone to come up with this commonly needed product?

Bedal Patch was designed by a David De Munter who at the time was a cancer patient who wanted to shower and was concerned about the risks for infection. Knowing the importance of keeping his exit site clean and dry at all times, David sought to develop a product that was specifically designed to allow patients to shower with or without their IV line connected.

About Bedal

    Bedal develops solutions to allow patients to shower autonomously with a connected intravenous catheter.
