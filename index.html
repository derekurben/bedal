Shower Safely with BedalPatch Comfort

BedalPatch Comfort

The newly patented and FDA registered Bedal Patch Comfort allows patients to safely shower during IV treatments. Unlike current solutions, the BedalPatch Comfort is specifically designed for patients to shower while connected or disconnected from their IV line, keeping the catheter exit site dry and minimizing the risk of infection.

The Bedal Patch Story

The BedalPatch was developed by David De Munter a cancer patient that lived through the challenges of showering with an indwelling catheter. Knowing the importance of keeping his exit site clean and dry at all times (approx. 25% of all catheter patients get and infection and approx. 12% of those die from it), David sought to develop a product that specifically allows patients to shower with or without their IV line connected.
