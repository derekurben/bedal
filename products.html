What is The Bedal Patch

Jigsaw Medical Products



The Bedel Patch Comfort:


● Is watertight dressing that allows a patient with a PICC, CVC Midline, PVC line to safely shower while connected or disconnected to their IV line.

● Is watertight dressing that allows a patient with a PICC, CVC Midline, PVC line to safely shower while connected or disconnected to their IV line.

● Is a comfortable to wear and allows for easy movement during showering.

● Is made of an elastic, highly breathable waterproof polyurethane film that minimizes condensation during showering.

● Is made of medical grade, skin friendly acrylic tape with high adhesion force and low skin irritation.

● Is a transparent polyurethane film, allowing visualization of the catheter site.

● Is a cost effective way to help protect from infections associated with IV therapy.

● Is easy to use and quickly removed after each shower.

● Is latex and DEHP-free.

● Is registered in the USA and manufactured of FDA approved materials.

● Is available today in the USA from Jigsaw Medical.


Features

Patient safety


Bedal Patch provides shielding of water to minimize the risk of allowing water to enter the catheter exit site during daily showing for patients undergoing IV therapy.


Easy to use


The Bedal Patch can be easily applied to patients and by patients after watching our short instructional video above. Additionally, once the Bedal Patch is removed and the dressing remains dry there is no need for a timely dressing change.


Stay connected to your IV line during your shower


The newly patented Bedal Patch was designed by renowned architect and cancer survivor David De Munter, to allow patients the ability to reduce the amount of unnecessary disconnections from their IV line.     Versatile Solution

    Patient safety


    The BedalPatch is a widely applicable solution, it can be used with PICC, CVC, Midline and PVC catheters, as well as with ports.

    One size fits all: all french sizes are accomodated, since the BedalPatch is fixed on the infusion line, with a standard diameter. It can be used with catheters with different french sizes.

    The BedalPatch is usable for different exit site locations, such as the arm and chest.
