What is The Bedal Patch

Medquest Products

The Bedel Patch Comfort:
  • A watertight dressing that allows a patient with a PICC, CVC Midline, PVC line to safely shower while connected or disconnected to their IV line.
  • Is comfortable to wear and allows for easy movement during showering.
  • Made of an elastic, highly breathable waterproof polyurethane film that minimizes condensation during showering.
  • Is made of medical grade, skin friendly acrylic tape with high adhesion force and low skin irritation. .
  • Our polyurethane film is transparent, allowing visualization of the catheter site.
  • Is a cost effective way to help protect from infections associated with IV therapy.
  • Easy to use and quickly removed after each shower.
  • Latex and DEHP-free.
  • Registered in the USA and manufactured of FDA approved materials.
  • Available today in the USA from Jigsaw Medical.

    • Features

    Patient safety

    Providing an adequate shielding of the exit site during washing to prevent water from entering.


    Easy to use

    Patients can apply the Bedalpacth themselves, without intervention from the nursing staff. No need to decouple the infusion line, thus interrupting the treatment and exposing the open ends to possible infections. No dressing changes needed on the exit site, so the washing will not interfere with the normal planned dressing changes.


    Patient autonomy

    The Bedalpatch makes the patient independent from the assistance of others in order to wash.


    Individually packaged

    Made in cleanrooms, to guarantee a dust and particle free clean product.


    Quality material

    The BedalPatch uses quality materials, for an optimal patient experience.

    • Highly breathable film
    • Soft touch, non plastic feel
    • Fingerlifts for easy handling and placing
    • Transparent for inspection of exit site
    • Large size to accommodate various exit sites

      • Versatile Solution

      Patient safety


      The BedalPatch is a widely applicable solution, it can be used with PICC, CVC, Midline and PVC catheters, as well as with ports.

      One size fits all: all french sizes are accomodated, since the BedalPatch is fixed on the infusion line, with a standard diameter. It can be used with catheters with different french sizes.

      The BedalPatch is usable for different exit site locations, such as the arm and chest.



      About Bedal

        Bedal develops solutions to allow patients to shower autonomously with a connected intravenous catheter.
